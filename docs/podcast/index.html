About

The Valve Wire Weekly is a podcast covering the latest developments in transcatheter valve technology — aortic (TAVR/TAVI), mitral (repair and replacement), and tricuspid therapies.

Each week, hosts Nolan Beckett and Claire discuss the latest research, clinical trial updates, regulatory developments, and industry analysis. We present balanced, circumspect analysis — always asking whether therapies have gotten ahead of the evidence, referencing both favorable findings and their limitations.

Our audience includes cardiac surgeons, interventional cardiologists, trainees, patients with valvular heart disease, and industry stakeholders.
