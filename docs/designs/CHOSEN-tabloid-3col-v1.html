TAVR Wins
On Mortality.
Loses On Durability.

The PARTNER 3 5-year extended follow-up published in NEJM this week shows a sustained mortality advantage for TAVR over SAVR in low-risk patients, but the structural valve deterioration signal in the TAVR arm at year 5 is the finding the device community is not talking about. Dr. Vinod Thourani's editorial calls for caution on extending the TAVR low-risk indication to patients under 65 until we see year 10 data. Edwards stock closed up 2.1% on the headline; the back-half of the paper is more sobering than the press release.

Mitral · EACTS

European Surgeons: MitraClip Not Ready For Prime Time

Position paper from a working group including Mehaffey and Chikwe says transcatheter mitral repair outcomes do not yet meet the threshold for routine use in surgical-eligible patients. Calls out durability data and the absence of head-to-head trials against contemporary surgical repair.

3 min read
FDA · Class I Recall

3,400 SAPIEN 3 Ultra Valves Pulled For Catheter Defect

Edwards Lifesciences disclosed the recall in an 8-K this week. The defect affects a small lot shipped January through March 2026. No patient harm reported. Manufacturing issue, not a device performance signal — but the FDA classification is the highest tier.
