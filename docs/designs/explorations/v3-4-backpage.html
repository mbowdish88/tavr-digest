TAVR valves deteriorating at year 5 — and nobody's talking about it
Structural Valve Deterioration — TAVR vs SAVR at 5 Years
Illustrative. Structural valve deterioration rate, PARTNER 3 extended follow-up.
The PARTNER 3 5-year extended follow-up published in NEJM shows a sustained mortality advantage for TAVR over SAVR in low-risk patients, but the structural valve deterioration signal in the TAVR arm at year 5 is the finding that the device community is not talking about. Dr. Vinod Thourani's editorial calls for caution on extending the TAVR low-risk indication to patients under 65 until we see year 10 data. Edwards stock closed up 2.1% on the headline; the back-half of the paper is more sobering than the press release.
